About Us

WHAT or WHO is PETTO ?

It’s the ultimate pet destination built exclusively for dogs and cats located in central of Kuala Lumpur. Our stunning six storey building is perfectly designed with special rooms and suites for your pets set with modern installations, a large indoor park, interaction play room and a rooftop pool area. It’s enough to make any dog or cat to wag its tail. Well, we also offer cat and dog boarding for your precious companions while you are away for work or vacation.
